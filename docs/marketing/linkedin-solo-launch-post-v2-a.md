Three things I shipped this week for EduSupervise.

So I built three things.

**1. Solo teacher path.**
You don't need a school admin to start.
Sign up. Run the 5-step wizard. Your own duty rotation in under 10 minutes.
205 teachers signed up in the first 30 days.

**2. Educational Assistants are a real user type now.**
They get assigned to specific slots.
No "mark complete" gate. Supervising is the whole job.

**3. PDF drop.**
If your school already has a duty rotation in PDF, drop it in.
The system reads it and builds the week in under 500 milliseconds.
No retyping. No AI vision hallucination. Just pdfplumber table extraction.
